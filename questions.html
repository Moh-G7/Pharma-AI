What is Pharma AI?

- Pharma AI is an app made for individuals having questions about medicine and pharmaceutical. Type in a question and the AI will answer it. The AI is made by a knowledge base which saves pre asked questions and already provided answers. This is important to know as questions need to be asked in specific. Pharma AI addresses the problem that individual has by making it an easy tool to use and get access of. Not being complicated this app is straightforward and formed for the users inquiries.
