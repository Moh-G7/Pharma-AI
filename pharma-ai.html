The Journey.

Pharma AI started off as an idea and developed into a website programmed and ran by HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I remember the idea came to me that in todays world there is a lot of people that don't know much about their wellnesss and health. The main goal of Pharma AI was to combat this issue by making an AI Chatbot available to answer common medical questions asked. The website would constantly be changed improving in design and the users experience.
